About Us

Power Wash Masters provides fast, reliable and cost-effective local power washing services for projects of all sizes in Charlotte, NC and surrounding areas. We pride ourselves on providing professional customer care because making our customers happy is always our number one goal. We offer a variety of services including but not limited to exterior pressure washing, soft washing, gutter cleaning and guard installation, deck and fence cleaning as well as general hardscape pressure washing.

Free Initial Quotes and More: We are happy to visit you property and explain what it would take to get the job done. Give us a call to learn more!

Who we serve: We proudly serve both residential and commercial customers in Charlotte, Weddington, Monroe, Matthews, Mint Hill, Ballantyne, Waxhaw and more with top-quality pressure washing services.

Supporting the community: Power Wash Masters proudly supports Changed Choices and Feeding America. Wonderful charities that support our community. Please help support our favorite local charities.
